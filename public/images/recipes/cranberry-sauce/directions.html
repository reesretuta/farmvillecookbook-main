Ingredients
  • 1 lb Frozen Cranberry
  • 1 tbs Orange Zest
  • 1 tsp Lemon Zest
  • 2 cups White Sugar
  • 1 Cinnamon Stick
  • 2 tbs Water
Directions
  1. Using a saucepan, combine frozen cranberries, sugar, cinnamon stick, salt and water.
  2. Cook on low heat and stir occasionally for around 10 minutes.
  3. Add the lemon and orange zest and cook on medium heat for 15 more minutes. Discard the cinnamon stick and wait for the sauce to cool before serving.
