Ingredients
  • 1 French Baguette
  • 1 cup Goat Cheese, softened
  • ½ tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Olive Oil
Directions
  1. Pre-heat oven to 350° F.
  2. Thinly slice the baguette and place on a sheet tray. Drizzle olive oil over the sliced baguette.
  3. Bake for 5 minutes or until crostini is golden and crispy.
  4. Spread goat cheese on crostini and finish with cracked black pepper and drizzled olive oil.
