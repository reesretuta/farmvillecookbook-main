Ingredients
  • 2 pieces White Bread
  • 2 tbs Apricot Preserves
  • 2 slices Fresh Peaches
  • 4 slices Jalapeño Pepper
  • 5 - 6 slices Ham
  • 2 slices Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • Butter for spreading and for the griddle
Directions
  1. Evenly spread a tablespoon of apricot preserves on both pieces of bread.
  2. Sandwich peaches, jalapeños, ham and cheddar slices in between bread.
  3. Evenly spread butter on outside of sandwich.
  4. Melt one tablespoon of butter on a griddle.
  5. Place the sandwich on the melted butter.
  6. Allow to cook for 3 - 5 minutes on each side or until the cheese has melted and bread is golden brown.
  7. Cut in half and enjoy!
