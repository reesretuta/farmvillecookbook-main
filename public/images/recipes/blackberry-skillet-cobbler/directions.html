Ingredients
  • 3 cups Blackberries
  • ½ cup Sugar, divided
  • 1 cup Flour
  • 1 cup Cornmeal
  • 6 tbs Butter, cold and cut into small pieces
  • 1 large Egg
  • ½ cup Milk with pinch of Salt
  • Extra Butter for skillet
Directions
  1. Pre-heat oven to 375º F.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine blackberries and ¼ cup sugar. Set aside.
  3. In another bowl, combine the flour, cornmeal, ¼ cup sugar and baking powder. Whisk together.
  4. Cut butter into flour mixture with a pastry cutter (or your fingers).
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg and milk. Then stir into the flour mixture.
  6. Butter the inside of a cast iron skillet.
  7. Pour blackberry and sugar mixture into skillet.
  8. Scoop dough mixture by the tablespoon and add on top of the blackberries.
  9. Bake for 30 minutes or until crust is golden brown.
