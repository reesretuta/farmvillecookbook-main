Ingredients
Chicken
  • 6 boneless Chicken Breasts
  • ½ cup Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1 cup Basil
  • ½ cup Olive Oil
  • 1 clove Garlic
Salsa
  • 3 Peaches, peeled and diced
  • ½ cup Red Onion, diced
  • ¼ cup Fresh Cilantro
  • 1 tbs Lime Juice
  • 1 tbs Honey
  • 1 tsp Balsamic Vinegar
  • ½ can Black Beans, drained and rinsed
Directions
Pesto Tomato Chicken:
  1. Dice the tomatoes and reduce gently in a frying pan with a little olive oil.
  2. Allow to cool and put into a food processor.
  3. Add the basil, garlic, and the rest of the olive oil and process until finely mixed.
  4. Lightly season the chicken breasts with fine sea salt and black pepper.
  5. Place the chicken in a mixing bowl and add the processed tomatoes, garlic and basil. Turn to ensure meat is well covered.
  6. Cover the mixing bowl and refrigerate for a minimum of 2 hours.
Peach Salsa:
  1. Combine the ingredients and set to one side.
  2. Preheat a greased grill to medium and cook chicken for about 6 minutes per side or until fully cooked.
  3. Serve the chicken with the salsa.
