Ingredients
  • 1 Tomato, diced
  • 1 Red Onion, diced
  • 1 Jalapeño, diced
  • 1 cup Cilantro, chopped
  • ½ Lemon, juiced
  • ½ Lime, juiced
  • ¼ cup Green Onion, chopped
  • ⅛ tsp Salt
  • ¼ tsp Black Pepper
Directions
  1. Prep all your herbs and vegetables.
  2. Combine and mix all the ingredients in a medium bowl, let it macerate for at least 30 minutes in the refrigerator prior to serving.
  3. Use as a salsa or pair with grilled fish and chicken dishes.
