Ingredients
  • 1 cup Whole Milk
  • ⅓ cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 tbs Butter
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • ½ tsp Pepper
  • ¼ tsp Garlic Powder
  • 5 - 8 Mint Leaves
  • ½ lb thin Spaghetti
  • ¾ cup fresh Green Peas
  • 4 small Rainbow Beets
Directions
  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Peel and thinly slice beets, then parboil them with the peas by dropping them in the water for about a minute. Remove and set aside.
  3. Bring the water back to a boil and add the pasta. Cook about 5 minutes or until just under al dente, then drain.
  4. While the pasta is cooking, combine the milk, cheese, butter, salt, pepper, and garlic powder in a sauté pan over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  5. When the sauce begins to boil, add the beets and peas and continue stirring for about 3 minutes.
  6. Thinly slice the mint and add it to the cream.
  7. Add the pasta to the cream, allowing it to finish cooking, absorbing some of the sauce, about 3 minutes.
  8. Plate and garnish with extra Parmesan, pepper, and mint if desired.
