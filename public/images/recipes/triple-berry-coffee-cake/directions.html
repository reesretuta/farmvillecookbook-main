Ingredients
  • 2 cups + ⅓ cup All-Purpose Flour, divided
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • ¼ cup Vegetable Oil
  • ¾ cup + ½ cup White Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • ½ cup Milk
  • ¼ cup Blueberries
  • ¼ cup Raspberries
  • ¼ cup Blackberries
  • ½ tsp ground Cinnamon
  • ¼ cup Butter, softened
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Grease and flour a 9 inch pan.
  2. Sift together the flour, baking powder and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the oil, sugar and egg. Stir in the flour mixture alternately with the milk, mixing just until incorporated. Fold in the berries. Pour batter into prepared pan.
  4. Cover with streusel topping: In a bowl, combine ⅓ cup flour, cinnamon and ½ cup sugar. Cut in the butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.
