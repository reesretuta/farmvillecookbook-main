Ingredients
Dough (9 ½ inch tart crust):
  • 1 cup All Purpose Flour
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • 1 lb Unsalted Butter, small diced
  • 3 tbs Cold Water
Filling:
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 4 Eggs
  • ½ cup Feta Cheese, crumbled
  • 1 tbs Garlic, minced
  • 1 Yellow Onion, diced
  • 6 cup Baby Spinach
  • 1 cup Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • ½ Black Pepper
Directions
Dough:
  1. Using a mixer or a food processor, add butter, flour and salt. Mix until you have a coarse meal mixture. Then add cold water.
  2. Pulse mixture until the dough is soft. Remove dough with your hands and form into a thick disk. Wrap in plastic and let it rest in the refrigerator for 2 hours.
  3. Move dough from refrigerator and wait until the dough is at room temperature before you start rolling.
  4. Sprinkle flour on a cool, dry surface and start rolling dough into a thin round disc, approximately 13 inches in diameter. Place the dough over your tart pan, allowing some time for it to relax. Trim the extra dough off the sides and use your fingers to press around the border of the pan, forming approximately ¼ inch ridges.
Filling:
  1. Preheat oven to 375º F degrees.
  2. In a medium sauté pan, melt butter over medium heat. Sauté garlic and onion until soft, then the add spinach and cook until soft and wilted. Add feta and cheddar cheeses, folding all ingredients until cheese is melted. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Wait until cool, and then spoon mixture into pie shell.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and milk.
  4. Pour liquid mixture into the pastry shell.
  5. Bake in oven for 35 - 40 minutes, or until it is set in center. Test by inserting a fork in the center. If the fork comes out clean, the quiche is ready.
  6. Rest quiche for at least 10 minutes before serving.
