Ingredients
  • 1 package (4 ounces) of Unsweetened Chocolate Chips
  • 1 cup Butter
  • 2 cups + ½ cup of Sugar, divided
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • ½ cup Cream Cheese, softened
  • 1 ¾ cups (10-ounce package) Hershey's Mini Kisses Milk Chocolates
  • ½ cup Red Maraschino Cherries, chopped
  • ½ tsp Almond Extract
Directions
  1. Heat oven to 350° F. Grease 13 x 9 x 2 baking pan.
  2. Place unsweetened chocolate and butter in large microwave–safe bowl. Microwave on medium (50%) for 2 minutes and 30 seconds, stirring after each minute, until mixture is melted.
  3. On medium, beat in 2 cups sugar, 3 eggs and vanilla until blended, add flour last until batter is evenly mixed.
  4. Spread batter in greased pan.
  5. In a separate mixing bowl, beat together cream cheese, remaining ½ cup sugar and remaining 1 egg.
  6. Stir in 1¼ cups of chocolate kisses, cherries and almond extract.
  7. Drop spoonfuls over top of chocolate mixture in pan. It does not need to be evenly spread over batter.
  8. Bake 35 - 40 minutes or until set. Remove from oven and immediately sprinkle remaining ½ cup of chocolates over the top. Cool completely in pan on wire rack. Once cooled, cut into bars.
