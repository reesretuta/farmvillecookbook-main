Ingredients
  • 4 cups fresh Basil Leaves
  • ½ cup Olive Oil
  • ¼ cup of Raw Pine Nuts
  • ¼ cup shredded Parmesan Cheese
  • ½ tbs Garlic, chopped
  • 1 tbs Lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • ½ tsp Black Pepper
Directions
  1. Combine basil leaves, olive oil, pine nuts, parmesan cheese, garlic, lemon juice, salt and pepper in a food processor.
  2. Process until pesto is smooth.
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve over pasta as a homemade sauce or use as a spread on sandwiches.
