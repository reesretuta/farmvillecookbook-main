Ingredients
Fish:
  • 1 Filet of Catfish (3 lbs)
  • 2 tbs of Sauté Oil
Seasoning:
  • 1 tbs Smoked Paprika
  • 2 tbs Celery Salt
  • 1 tbs Garlic Powder
  • 2 tbs Sugar
  • 1 tbs Onion Powder
  • ½ tbs Ground Cayenne Pepper
  • 2 tbs Black Pepper
  • ½ tbs Coriander
  • 1 tbs Mustard Powder
Directions
  1. Mix together the smoked paprika, salt, garlic powder, sugar, onion powder, ground cayenne pepper, black pepper, coriander and mustard powder in a small bowl.
  2. Pat the catfish dry and apply mixed spices liberally on the catfish filet.
  3. Heat the sauté oil in a skillet or sauté pan on medium-high until hot.
  4. Place seasoned fish in pan and sear each side for 3 minutes.
