Ingredients
Cupcakes:
  • 2 Eggs, separated
  • 1 ¾ cups Cake Flour, sifted
  • 2 tbs Baking Powder
  • ¼ tsp Salt
  • ½ cup Butter, room temperature
  • ⅔ cup + ¼ cup Sugar, divided
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • ½ cup Milk
  • ⅛ tsp Cream of Tartar
Mint Frosting:
  • ½ cup Unsalted Butter, room temperature
  • 3 - 4 cups Confectioner’s Sugar
  • ¼ cup Milk
  • ½ tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
  • ⅛ tsp Pure Peppermint Extract
Directions
Cupcakes:
  1. Separate eggs when they’re cold. Cover and let them come to room temperature.
  2. Beat butter until soft, add larger amount of sugar and continue beating until light and fluffy. Slowly add egg yolks and vanilla. Mix until fully incorporated.
  3. With the mixer on low, alternately add dry ingredients and milk - beginning and ending with flour.
  4. In a clean bowl, whisk egg whites and cream of tartar until they hold soft peaks. Gradually add the smaller amount of sugar and continue beating until they hold stiff peaks.
  5. With a spatula fold egg whites into cake batter.
  6. Bake at 350º F for 20 minutes.
Mint Frosting:
  1. In the bowl of an electric mixer, cream butter until smooth and creamy, 2 - 3 minutes.
  2. With mixer on low speed, add 3 cups sugar, milk, and both extracts; mix until light and fluffy. If necessary, gradually add remaining cup of sugar to reach desired consistency.
