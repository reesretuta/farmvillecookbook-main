Ingredients
  • 20 small Red Potatoes
  • 1 tbs Olive Oil
  • ½ cup Cream Cheese Spread
  • 1 tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 4 cooked Bacon Strips, chopped
  • 1 cup Monterey Jack Cheese, finely shredded
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • ½ tsp Black Pepper
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 450º F.
  2. In a bowl, toss potatoes in olive oil, salt and black pepper then transfer potatoes to a baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes, or until you can easily pierce with a fork. Let it rest until cool.
  4. While potatoes are in the oven, finely shred the Monterey Jack cheese and chop the bacon.
  5. When potatoes are cool, cut the top of the potatoes with a paring knife. Then use a melon baller or small spoon to scoop out the center of the potatoes. Save the scooped potato for the filling.
  6. Mix together cream cheese, Italian seasoning, bacon, Monterey Jack cheese, and the scooped potato. Set aside.
  7. Fill potatoes with the filling mixture then place back in oven for an additional 8 minutes.
