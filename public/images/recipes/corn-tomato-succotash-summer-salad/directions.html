Ingredients
  • 1 quart fresh cut Bread and Butter Corn Kernels
  • ½ pint Heirloom Cherry Tomatoes, halved
  • ½ cup Sweet Red Onion, diced
  • ½ cup Carrot, diced and blanched
  • 1 pint Fresh Romano Beans, sliced and blanched
  • 1 bunch Chives, cut 1 inch
  • ¼ cup Chiffonade Basil
  • ½ cup Feta Cheese, crumbled
  • 1 Lemon, juice and zest
  • 3 tbs Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
Directions
  1. Prep the vegetables.
  2. In a large bowl, mix corn, cherry tomatoes, red onion, carrot, and romano beans together.
  3. Add lemon juice, lemon zest, olive oil, salt and pepper; mix together.
  4. Add feta cheese and basil; lightly toss.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
