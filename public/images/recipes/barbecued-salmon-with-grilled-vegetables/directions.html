Ingredients
Salmon:
  • 1 Salmon fillet with skin
  • Olive Oil cooking spray
  • 1 ½ tsp of Olive Oil per Salmon Filet
  • 2 - 3 Raw Onion Rings per Salmon Filet
  • 1 Lemon, cut into round slices
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
Vegetables:
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper, cut in large square pieces
  • 1 Yellow or Orange Bell Pepper, cut in large square pieces
  • 1 Onion, sliced into ¼ inch rounds
  • 1 container of fresh Mushrooms, halved
  • 1 head of Broccoli, cut into pieces
  • ½ cup Olive Oil
  • 2 tbs Balsamic Vinegar
  • 1 tbs Montreal Steak Seasoning
Directions
Salmon:
  1. Spray a large piece of aluminum foil with cooking spray and place salmon on foil, skin down.
  2. Drizzle salmon with olive oil, season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cover with sliced onions and sliced lemon.
  4. Close aluminum foil and wrap with an extra piece of foil.
  5. Cook on BBQ at medium heat for about 20 minutes, depending on size and thickness of salmon.
Vegetables:
  1. Prep the vegetables.
  2. Put all cut vegetables in a plastic container and sprinkle with olive oil, balsamic vinegar and Montreal steak seasoning.
  3. Put cover on plastic container, shake well and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Cook in a wok on the BBQ for 15 - 20 minutes at high heat, stirring often.
