Ingredients
  • 2 Bell Peppers
  • 1 cup Moroccan Couscous
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 cup Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 2 tbs Parsley, chopped
  • 1 Orange, zest and juice
  • 1 tbs Fresno Chili, chopped
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, chopped
  • ½ Yellow Onion, small diced
  • ½ cup Zucchini, small diced
  • ½ cup Tomato, small diced
  • ½ cup Green Onion, chopped
  • 2 tbs Olive Oil
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • ¼ tsp Cumin
  • ¼ tsp Smoked Paprika
Directions
  1. Pre-heat oven to 350º F.
  2. Cut the top off the bell peppers. Remove all the seeds and stems. Cut the top into small cubes.
  3. Blanch bell peppers for 3 minutes in salted boiling water.
  4. Hydrate couscous with boiling water. Once it is hydrated, fluff couscous using a fork.
  5. Sauté onions and garlic in hot oil and add the remaining vegetables in the following order: diced bell pepper, zucchini, and tomatoes. Season with cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper. Add couscous and fold into vegetables.
  6. Fill the peppers with couscous mixture and top with sharp cheddar cheese.
  7. Bake for 15 minutes.
  8. Enjoy it!
