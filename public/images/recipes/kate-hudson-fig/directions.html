Ingredients
  • 1 ½ cup All Purpose Flour
  • ½ cup Water
  • ⅓ cup Olive Oil
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • 1 whole Burrata
  • 10 Figs, sliced in quarters
  • 1 cup Arugula
  • 1 tbs Honey
  • ½ tsp Fleur De Sel
Directions
  1. Pre-heat oven to 450° F.
  2. Using medium bowl, mix together water and salt. While mixing, slowly add the olive oil.
  3. Add ¾ of the flour and whisk together the mixture until you have elastic sticky dough.
  4. Gradually add the rest of the flour until you get workable dough.
  5. Roll the dough into a circle using a roller, not making the dough too thin. Note: it does not need to be perfectly round.
  6. Bake in greased pan for 8 - 10 minutes or until dry and crispy.
  7. Brush the flatbread with olive oil and let it completely cool - otherwise your cheese will melt.
  8. Top flatbread with burrata cheese mounds, sliced figs and arugula.
  9. Finish with a drizzle of honey and a sprinkle of fleur de sel.
  10. Slice flatbread into squares and enjoy!
