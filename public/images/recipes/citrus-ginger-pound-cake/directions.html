Ingredients
Cake Ingredients:
  • 1 ½ cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 cup Plain Greek Yogurt
  • ¾ cup Sugar
  • 3 tbs Brown Sugar
  • 2¼ tsp Baking Powder
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1 tsp Fresh Ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp Orange Zest
  • 1 tsp Lemon Zest
  • ⅓ cup Coconut Oil
Glaze Ingredients:
  • ¼ cup Raw or Turbinado Sugar
  • ¼ cup fresh squeezed Orange Juice
Directions
  1. Preheat the oven to 350° F.
  2. Combine flour, baking powder and salt in a small bowl.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk together the yogurt, eggs, oil, vanilla, ginger, orange zest, lemon zest, brown sugar and 1 cup of sugar.
  4. Slowly mix the dry into the wet ingredients and continue to whisk until the ingredients are combined well.
  5. Pour into a greased 8 × 4 bread pan and bake for 40 minutes.
  6. Slice the oranges about ¼ inch thick.
  7. Heat ½ sugar and orange juice over medium heat and stir until all the sugar is dissolved.
  8. Bring sugar mixture to a boil, and then add the orange slices. Reduce the heat to low and allow to cook for about 20 minutes.
  9. Remove the oranges and place on a wire rack to cool.
  10. Place the cake on a wire rack over a plate to cool. Poke holes in the top with a toothpick. Allow to cool completely.
  11. Whisk together the powdered sugar and 2 tablespoons of orange juice and pour over the cooled cake. As it drips onto the plate below, you can spoon it over the cake a few times.
  12. Place the candied oranges on top and enjoy!
