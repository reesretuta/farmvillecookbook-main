Ingredients
  • 2 Eggplants
  • 3 cups Panko Breadcrumbs
  • 3 cups All Purpose Flour
  • 2 Eggs, whisked
  • 3 cups Parmesan Cheese, grated
  • 6 cups Tomato Sauce
  • 1 cup Fresh Basil
  • 6 slices of Buffalo Mozzarella
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 3 cups Canola Oil, for frying
  • 1 tsp Olive Oil
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 375° F.
  2. Slice Eggplant into 2 inch thick round slices.
  3. In a mixing bowl, mix flour and salt together.
  4. In a second mixing bowl, whisk together eggs.
  5. In a third mixing bowl, add Panko Breadcrumbs.
  6. Cover the eggplant slices by dipping in the flour mixture first, then whisked eggs, and finally in panko breadcrumbs covering the eggplant slices entirely.
  7. Heat up oil on medium heat using a sauté pan. Fry the breaded eggplant for 2 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
  8. Place one eggplant slice in a baking dish, cover with a layer of tomato sauce and mozzarella. Repeat the process by layering a second slice of eggplant on top and covering with tomato sauce and mozzarella.
  9. Place the baking dish in the oven for 15 - 20 minutes. Remove when the cheese is golden brown.
  10. Garnish with basil, tossed in olive oil and Parmesan cheese.
