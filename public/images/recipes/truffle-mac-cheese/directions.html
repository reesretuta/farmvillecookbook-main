Ingredients
  • 8 cups Elbow Macaroni, uncooked
  • 3 cups Sharp Cheddar Cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup Sour Cream
  • ½ cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 4 cups Whole Milk
  • 2 tbs Butter, unsalted
  • 2 tbs All Purpose Flour
  • 1 tbs Truffle Oil
  • ½ cup Bread Crumbs
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • ½ tsp Black Pepper
Directions
  1. Pre-heat oven to 350º F.
  2. Cook Pasta al dente in boiled and salted water. Drain.
  3. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour to make a thick sauce. Add milk to sauce slowly, continuously stirring.
  4. Stir in cheeses until completely melted and you have a thick velvet sauce.
  5. Add the macaroni to the saucepan. Using a spatula, fold the noodles into the sauce. Add truffle oil.
  6. Transfer the macaroni to a casserole dish.
  7. Toast bread crumbs in a skillet over medium heat until breadcrumbs are golden.
  8. Spread bread crumbs over the macaroni & cheese casserole.
  9. Bake in oven for 30 minutes.
