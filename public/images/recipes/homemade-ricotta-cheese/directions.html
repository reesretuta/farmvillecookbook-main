Ingredients
  • 4 cups Whole Milk
  • ⅔ tsp Salt
  • 2 tbs Lemon Juice
Directions
  1. Add milk and salt to a medium thick-bottomed pan and gently bring to a boil.
  2. Stir occasionally until it begins to boil, then turn off the heat and add lemon juice.
  3. Stir a few times and allow it to fully curdle – it takes about a minute.
  4. Lay the cheesecloth in a fine strainer over a bowl.
  5. Pour the mixture over the cheesecloth and let it drain for a few minutes. The longer it drains, the drier it will be. Let it drain for about 10 - 20 minutes for a moister cheese.
  6. Drizzle with honey and serve with crackers or fresh sliced fruit or add to favorite pastas or lasagnas.
