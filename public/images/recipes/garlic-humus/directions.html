Ingredients
  • 2 cups Garbanzo Bean, rinsed and drained
  • ½ cup Olive Oil
  • 5 tbs Lemon Juice
  • 2 tbs Tahini
  • 1 tbs Garlic, chopped
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • ¼ tsp Black Pepper
Directions
  1. Combine garbanzo beans, lemon juice, olive oil, tahini, garlic, salt and black pepper in a food processer.
  2. Process until hummus is smooth.
  3. Serve with pita bread or fresh vegetables.
