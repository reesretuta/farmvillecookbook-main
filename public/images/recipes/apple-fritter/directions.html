Ingredients
  • 2 tbs Butter, melted
  • ½ cup Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 2¼ tsp Baking Powder
  • 2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1¼ tsp Salt
  • ¾ cups Milk
  • 2 Apples, peeled and diced
Directions
  1. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon and salt. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk eggs, then add milk, vanilla and cooled melted butter. Mix together.
  3. Gently fold dry ingredients into wet, mixing until combined. Fold in apples. The batter should be very chunky.
  4. Lay parchment paper down onto sheet trays and spray with a thin coat of pan spray.
  5. Using an ice cream scoop, portion fritters onto parchment paper.
  6. Heat oil to 350° F and fry for about 2 minutes each or until batter is cooked all the way through.
  7. Remove from oil and drain onto paper towels.
  8. Immediately toss in powdered sugar glaze, or sprinkle with powdered sugar.
  9. Serve warm.
