Ingredients
  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 ¼ cups Cake Flour
  • 1 tbs Baking Powder
  • ½ tsp Baking Soda
  • ½ cup Sugar
  • 2 lbs Unsalted Butter
  • ½ cup Heavy Cream
  • ½ cup Pumpkin Puree
Directions
  1. Heat oven to 350° F. Grease a 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking pan.
  2. Sift all-purpose flour, cake flour, baking powder, baking soda and sugar together.
  3. Add butter and mix until there are no clumps.
  4. With the mixer on low, slowly pour in cream.
  5. Add pumpkin and mix for 30 seconds until all of the dry ingredients are moistened and the dough comes together around the paddle.
  6. Shape dough and cut into desired shapes.
  7. Brush scones with cream and sprinkle heavily with sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Bake at 325° F for 15 -18 minutes.
