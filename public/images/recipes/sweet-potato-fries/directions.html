Ingredients
  • 1 Sweet Potato, large
  • 1 tbs Sauté Oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Garlic Powder to taste
  • Chipotle Powder to taste
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 350º F.
  2. Cut the sweet potato lengthwise into 6 - 8 wedges.
  3. Lightly coat wedges with the sauté oil.
  4. Place wedges on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake in oven for 20 minutes, or until slightly browned and crispy.
  6. Remove from the oven and season with salt, pepper, garlic powder and chipotle powder to taste.
