Ingredients
  • 2 lbs Chicken Breast, boneless, skinless
  • 5 slices of Bacon
  • 3 tbs Brown Sugar
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • ½ Black Pepper
Directions
  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high.
  2. Cut the chicken into 1.5 inch squares and season chicken with salt and black pepper.
  3. Thread the bacon and chicken onto the skewer, working at the top of the skewer and pushing the meat down as you go. Repeat.
  4. Sprinkle half of the brown sugar over one side of the raw meat.
  5. Carefully place the skewers on the grill, brown sugar side down.
  6. Sprinkle the remaining brown sugar over the skewers and cook for 6 - 8 minutes.
  7. Flip the skewers over and cook for an additional 6 - 10 minutes, until the chicken reaches 165º F on an instant read thermometer.
  8. Remove the skewers from the grill when cooked and let them rest for 5 minutes.
  9. Serve while they are still hot.
