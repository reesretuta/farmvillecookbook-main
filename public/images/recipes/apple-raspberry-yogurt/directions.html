Ingredients
  • 4 cups Plain Yogurt
  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 ½ cups Heavy Cream
  • ½ cup Fresh Raspberries, chopped or mashed if desired
  • 1 Apple, diced small
Directions
  1. Whisk the yogurt, raspberries, apples and sugar together.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the heavy cream until it forms stiff peaks.
  3. Gradually fold the heavy cream into the yogurt mixture to create a mousse-like consistency.
  4. Pour the yogurt mixture into desired ice pop molds and freeze until firm for at least 30 minutes.
