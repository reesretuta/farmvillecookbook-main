Ingredients
  • ½ cup Corn Meal
  • ½ cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • ½ cup Buttermilk
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Shallot, diced
  • ½ cup Green Onion, chopped
  • 1 ½ cup Kernel Corn
  • 1 cup Canola Oil, for frying
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tbs Unsalted Butter
Directions
  1. Using a medium bowl, mix together corn meal, flour, baking powder and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat together egg and buttermilk. Then add to the flour mixture.
  3. In a skillet over medium heat, sauté corn and shallots until soft, then add the chopped green onions. Let cool and add to the batter mixture.
  4. Heat oil in a heavy pot or deep fryer to 365° F and drop your fritter batter by the spoonful into the hot oil, and fry until golden. Drain onto paper towels until ready to eat.
