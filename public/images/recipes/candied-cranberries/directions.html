Ingredients
  • 2 cups White Sugar
  • 2 cups Water
  • 3 cups Fresh Cranberries
  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar
Directions
  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine sugar and water until it reaches the consistency of simple syrup.
  2. Allow simple syrup to cool completely.
  3. Add cranberries to the simple syrup and transfer to a rack to drain off the excess liquid.
  4. Roll cranberries in granulated sugar.
  5. Set aside. Allow to cool and harden before serving.
