Ingredients
  • 1 Whole Trout, cleaned
  • 2 tbs of Canola Oil
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • 2 tbs All Purpose Flour
Directions
  1. Season trout with salt and pepper, then pat with flour.
  2. Heat the canola oil in a skillet or sauté pan on medium-high until hot.
  3. Place fish in the pan, skin down. Sear each side for 3 minutes or until golden and cooked through.
