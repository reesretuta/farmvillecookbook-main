Ingredients
Coffee Cake:
  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 ½ tsp Baking Powder
  • ¼ tsp Salt
  • ¼ cup Butter, room temperature
  • ½ cup Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • ½ tsp Vanilla Extract
  • ⅓ cup Milk
  • 2 cups Blueberries (optional)
Streusel Topping:
  • ⅓ cup All-Purpose Flour
  • ⅓ cup Sugar
  • ½ tsp Cinnamon
  • ¼ cup Butter, cold cubed
Directions
  1. Whisk flour, baking powder and salt together. Set aside.
  2. Beat butter until smooth. Add sugar and beat until light and fluffy. Scrape bowl and add eggs slowly, beating until incorporated after each addition. Add vanilla extract and mix in.
  3. Starting and ending with flour mixture, alternately add milk and flour, mix and repeat twice.
  4. Spread batter on the bottom of prepared 12 x 12 pan and evenly distribute blueberries over the top.
  5. Make streusel topping by mixing dry ingredients together, then cutting in the butter. Sprinkle over blueberries.
  6. Bake at 350° F for 30 - 40 minutes.
