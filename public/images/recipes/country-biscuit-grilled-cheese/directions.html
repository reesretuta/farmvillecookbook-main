Ingredients
  • 3 ¾ cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 tbs Granulated Sugar
  • 4 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 cup Butter, cold and cubed
  • 1 cup Buttermilk
    • Grilled Cheese:
    • 3 - 4 Tomato (2 slices per sandwich)
    • Sliced Cheese (Cheddar or Gruyère)
    • Salt and Pepper, to taste
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 375º F.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and baking soda.
  3. Cut in butter until crumbly. Then add buttermilk to moisten until it becomes dough.
  4. Roll out dough on floured surface and cut into desired size and shape – roughly the size of an English muffin.
  5. Place on baking sheet and bake for 15 minutes, or until golden brown.
  6. Cut the biscuits in half and toast so that the inside is lightly browned.
  7. Create a sandwich with the desired amount of cheese between two slices of tomato. In a non-stick pan, sauté each sides of the tomatoes just like you would the bread of a grilled cheese. Salt and pepper each side of the tomatoes prior to sautéing.
  8. Once the cheese is melted between the tomatoes, place seasoned tomato sandwich in between the toasted biscuit.
  9. Place back in oven for 2 - 3 minutes and serve while hot.
