Ingredients
  • ½ cup Butter, room temperature
  • ½ cup + 1 tbs Brown Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • ½ tsp Vanilla Extract
  • ¾ cup All-Purpose Flour
  • ½ tsp Baking Soda
  • ½ tsp Cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp Salt
  • 2 cups Oats
  • ¾ cup Raisins
  • ½ cup Chocolate Chips
Directions
  1. Mix butter and brown sugar together until light and fluffy. Scrape down the sides of the bowl. Then add vanilla and eggs, mixing in one at a time.
  2. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl and stir in all-purpose flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt. Mix until combined.
  3. Stir in oats, chocolate chips and raisins.
  4. Bake at 350° F for 10 minutes.
