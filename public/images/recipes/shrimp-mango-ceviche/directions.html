Ingredients
Ceviche:
  • 2 lbs Cooked Shrimp
  • 1 Mango, diced
  • ½ Medium Purple Onion, Sliced
  • ¼ cup Chopped Cilantro
  • 1 tbs Capers
Ceviche Dressing:
  • ½ cup Olive Oil
  • ¼ cup Vinegar
  • Juice of 1 Lemon
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 2 oz Caper Juice (about ¼ of the jar)
Directions
  1. Whisk all dressing ingredients together in a bowl. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl add the shrimp, onion, capers, cilantro and mango.
  3. Toss the dressing in with the shrimp.
  4. Let it marinate in the fridge for a minimum of 2 hours.
