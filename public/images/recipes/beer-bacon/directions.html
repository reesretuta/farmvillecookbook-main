Ingredients
  • 1 ½ cup Cornmeal
  • 1 ½ cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 tbs Sugar
  • 1½ tbs Baking Powder
  • ¾ tbs Salt
  • 1 ½ cup Sharp Cheddar Cheese, shredded (set aside 1 cup for topping)
  • 3 large Eggs
  • ½ cup Milk
  • ⅓ cup Sour Cream
  • 1 cup Beer
  • ½ cup Butter, melted
  • 6 slices Thick Cut Bacon, chopped and fried
Directions
  1. In a mixer, combine cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and shredded cheese.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine eggs, milk, sour cream, beer, and melted butter.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients in the mixer until combined, and then add the bacon.
  4. Pour into a greased 9 x 13 inch cake pan. Spread the batter evenly.
  5. Add the remaining 1 cup of shredded sharp cheddar cheese to the top, spreading evenly.
  6. Bake at 375° F for 30 - 35 minutes.
  7. Allow to cool for 10 minutes before serving.
