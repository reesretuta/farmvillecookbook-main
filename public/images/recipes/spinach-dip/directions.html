Ingredients
  • 1 Yellow Onion, diced
  • ½ cup Olive Oil
  • 2 lbs Spinach, cleaned
  • ¼ cup shredded Parmesan Cheese
  • ½ cup Cream Cheese
  • ¼ cup Mozzarella, shredded
  • ½ cup Whole Milk
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • ½ tbs Garlic, chopped
  • 1 tbs Lemon Juice
  • ½ tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tbs Butter, unsalted
  • 1 tsp Salt
Directions
  1. Pre-heat oven 350° F degrees.
  2. In a sauté pan on medium heat melt the butter, add garlic and onion and cook until softened. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
  3. When spinach done, remove from heat and allow to cool.
  4. Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl except for the Parmesan cheese. Once mixed, transfer mixture to the baking dish.
  5. Bake dip for 10 minutes, pull out of the oven and top with Parmesan cheese.
  6. Place dip back in the oven for an additional 10 minutes or until cheese is golden.
