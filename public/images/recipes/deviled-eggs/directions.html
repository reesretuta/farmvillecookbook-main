Ingredients
  • 12 Eggs
  • ½ cup Mayonnaise
  • 1 tbs Dijon Mustard
  • ⅓ tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • ⅓ tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp White Wine Vinegar
  • 2 cups of Ice Cubes
  • ⅓ tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Paprika
Directions
  1. Place eggs in a single layer saucepan; cover eggs with cold water, about 1 inch.
  2. Using high heat, bring water to a boil and cook for 1 minute. Take the pot off the heat and cover saucepan with a lid. Allow eggs to sit in the hot water for 10 minutes.
  3. Transfer your eggs to a bowl of ice water and let them cool for 5 minutes.
  4. Under running water, carefully crack and peel your eggs.
  5. To prepare the filing, slice eggs lengthwise and carefully remove the yolks. Set the whites aside.
  6. Using a mixing bowl, mash yolks, Dijon mustard, vinegar, paprika, mayonnaise, salt and black pepper and until you have a smooth mixture.
  7. Transfer filing to a piping bag with a large star or round tip and pipe the mixture into the whites. Garnish with herbs of your preference or a dash of cayenne pepper.
