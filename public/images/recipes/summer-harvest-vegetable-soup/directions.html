Ingredients
  • 4 cups Vegetable Stock
  • 1 cup Yellow Onion, diced
  • 1 cup Zucchini, chopped
  • 1 cup Yellow Summer Squash, chopped
  • 1 cup Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1 cup Carrot, sliced
  • 1 cup Baby Spinach
  • 1 tsp Garlic, chopped
  • ½ cup Green Beans
  • ⅓ cup Dry Cannellini Bean
  • 1 tbs Red Wine Vinegar
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • ½ tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tbs Parmesan cheese, garnish
  • 1 tbs Parsley, chopped
Directions
  1. Use a 6-quart slow cooker.
  2. Place your vegetables and beans into the slow cooker.
  3. Add vegetable stock, red wine vinegar, salt and black pepper.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 8 hours, or until beans have reached desired tenderness.
  5. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley.
